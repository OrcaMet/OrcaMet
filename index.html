Why Orcamet?

Orcamet delivers hyper-accurate, industry-specific weather forecasting using state-of-the-art models and proprietary data. Whether you're on land, at sea, or operating hundreds of feet in the air, we ensure your operations stay safe, productive, and predictive.

Industries We Serve

Farming & Agriculture

From microclimate field-level forecasts to seasonal rain probability modeling, Orcamet empowers farmers with the precision they need to optimize crop cycles, irrigation, and harvesting. Reduce risk, prevent yield loss, and make smarter agronomic decisions.

High-Risk & Elevated Work

Work-at-height (WAH), crane operators, and facade engineers rely on Orcamet to identify dangerous wind patterns, sudden thermal shifts, and lightning risks. Our systems provide real-time alerts to protect lives and prevent costly work stoppages in high-rise and hazardous industries.

Marine & Offshore

Navigate confidently with Orcamet’s marine forecasting — from tidal and wave forecasts to offshore wind predictions and swell warnings. Trusted by fishermen, shipping fleets, offshore platforms, and marine researchers worldwide.
