High-Risk Work Environments

From skyscraper scaffolding to offshore oil rigs, safety is paramount. Orcamet's real-time wind, lightning, and precipitation alerts help you minimize risk and enforce site shutdowns with confidence. Every forecast includes a dynamic risk scale tailored for WAH (Working At Height) operations and can integrate with any on site weather stations.
