Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 July 2025

1. Who We Are

OrcaMet provides weather forecasting services for marine, farming, and high-risk industries. This privacy policy outlines how we handle any personal data we collect from you or that you provide to us.

2. What Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide (name, email, phone) when making enquiries or using our services
  • Usage data from our website (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Any data you send to us when working together (e.g. GPS tracks, weather station logs)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide requested services
  • To maintain internal records and client communication
  • To analyse website usage and improve the user experience

4. Data Sharing

We will never sell your data. We only share your data with trusted third parties where necessary to deliver services (e.g. cloud storage, analytics) and only under appropriate data protection agreements.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in the UK or EU. We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect it from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies to monitor site performance. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on it
