Marine & Offshore Forecasting

Precision forecasts for confident operations at sea, built for offshore industries, maritime logistics, and coastal safety.

Forecasting Built for Marine Decision-Making

Orcamet's marine forecasting solutions are developed to support real-time, high-stakes decisions across offshore platforms, shipping operations, and coastal infrastructure. With fine-scale resolution, we deliver trusted insight into wave height, gusts, swell patterns, wind, and tide data.

Key Features:

  • High-resolution wave and swell modeling
  • Integration with vessel and fleet tracking systems
  • Wind alerts tailored for crane lifts and port entry
  • Forecast updates when you need them
  • Real-time lightning and squall monitoring

Use Cases:

From offshore wind construction to regular oil rig operations, Orcamet's tools adapt to your mission profile and vessel risk profile.
